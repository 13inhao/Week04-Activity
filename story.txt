He awoke to the huge, insect like creatures looming over his bed and screamed his lungs out. 
They hastily left the room and he stayed up all night, shaking and wondering if it had been a dream.
His roommates assured him that everything was fine, and he went back to sleep. Soon after the creatures crept out of the dark oncemore.
After this second rude awakening, he did discover, however, that the beings were easily distracted by any source of light. Thusly, he opened his window, letting in the bright moonlight, and the creatures flew off into the sky.
